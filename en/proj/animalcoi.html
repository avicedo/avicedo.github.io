Poster for Animalcoi

This was my proposal for the poster of the second edition of the animation festival "Animalcoi", which was chosen through a contest my work being awarded the runner-up prize.

The poster is based on a concept closely linked to the world of animation, the freeze frames. These images are those that are positioned between the key frames of a movement to make it more fluid.
From this concept I used the festival's mascot, Aitana, and faked its movement in these freeze frames to finally create a closed and modular composition, for easy adaptation to other formats.
