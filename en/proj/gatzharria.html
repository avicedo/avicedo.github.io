Gatz-Harria Restaurant

Gatz-Harria is a faux project in which the graphic image of a pintxos restaurant located in Donostia was created. Specializing in fusion cuisine, Gatz-Harria seeks to combine the traditional taste of Donostia's tapas, "pintxos", with a modern spirit.
We are looking to develop a brand that is able to adapt to different areas, from packaging to a web environment without any problem.

In order to create the brand image of a pintxo restaurant, one of our main objectives was to combine the classic references of the Basque "herriko tabernas" with a more modern and clean aesthetic style, to bring the concepts transmitted by the traditional Basque bar cuisine to an environment closer to big city establishments.

Among the elements requested by the client, we developed the naming of the restaurant along with its logo and variants. We also established the business cards of the establishment, the menus and the placemats that customers would use.
