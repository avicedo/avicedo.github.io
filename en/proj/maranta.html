Maranta Films

The Valencian film production company Maranta Maranta was born 4 years ago by two graduates of Audiovisual Communication, lovers of human stories and the documentary format. Since their debut with the short film «Mi vida después del fuego», they have focused their production on works that tell stories seen from a very personal and close point of view.
In 2023 they contacted me to develop the redesign of their brand based on the values

that characterize them: Humanity, dedication, approachability and humor.
The restyling was based on these values, seeking to create a familiar and friendly brand. As we developed the logo, we decided to sintetize the shape of the maranta leaf to a flat geometry and, from that point on, we developed the rest of the characters in a modular way.
Then we developed the requested branding applications and a complete font based on the characters of the logotype.

Chromatically we changed the palette to give the new brand more versatility and brightness, since the old color palette, centered around browns, reds and greens, darkened the overall image.
For this we made a 180º change and based our applications on white and a combination of four pastel colors that can be joined by gradients.
